What Can I Do As A Youth Abolitionist?

Abolitionists do not have uniform ideas on how to abolish the current system. They focus their energy on efforts that they believe are most effective or address a specific problem. Read more about some of the many ways to get involved.

PUBLIC DEMONSTRATIONS

Support abolition by attending public demonstrations that demand changes such as, defunding the police, defunding ICE, or abolishing prisons.

MUTUAL AID MAP

MUTUAL AID

Give and receive help by joining mutual aid practices to help create mutually beneficial relationships in your community.

SHARE

Share abolitionist efforts through accessible platforms (word of mouth, classroom, social media, etc.). Make clear that abolition is not a trend, it is an ongoing movement.

EDUCATION

Educate yourself and teach others about abolition. Question societal values and the system we live under.
