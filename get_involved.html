What Can I Do As A Youth Abolitionist?

Abolitionists do not have uniform ideas on how to abolish the current system. They focus their energy on efforts that they believe are most effective or address a specific problem. Read more about some of the many ways to get involved.

Public Demonstration

Support abolition attending public demonstrations that demand changes such as, defunding the police, defunding ICE, or abolishing prisons.
